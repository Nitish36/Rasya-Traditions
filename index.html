About Us

At Rasya Tradition, every saree is handled with patience, care, and deep respect for the fabric. With a strong understanding of different saree types, textures, and traditional folding styles, we ensure each saree is carefully ironed and folded to preserve its beauty and longevity. From everyday wear to treasured silk sarees meant for special occasions, our work reflects precision, consistency, and a genuine love for the craft. Every pleat, edge, and layer is handled with attention to detail, ensuring a neat and elegant finish. Saree ironing and folding is not just a service — it is a skill that requires experience, fabric knowledge, and careful handling. We specialize in professional saree ironing and traditional folding techniques, treating every saree with the care it deserves.

What We Offer


Saree Ironing

Professional saree ironing with careful temperature control and fabric-specific handling to ensure a smooth, crease-free finish.

Box Fold

Saree is neatly ironed and folded into a rectangle

Easy to stack in cupboards

Most common for daily-wear sarees

Pleated Fold

Pallu and pleats aligned properly

Maintains saree structure

Preferred for silk and festive sarees

Pallu-Visible Fold

Pallu design kept visible on top

Ideal for choosing sarees without unfolding

Often used in boutiques



Roll Fold

Saree rolled instead of folded

Prevents deep crease lines

Useful for travel or delicate fabrics

Box Fold

Structured folding with sharp edges

Used for gifting or premium storage

Looks very polished

Silk-Care Fold

Extra care to avoid harsh creases

Sometimes folded with tissue/cloth layers

Ideal for Kanchipuram, Banarasi, Mysore silk

Saree Pinning

Saree pinning is done with precision to ensure the saree stays secure, comfortable, and neatly draped throughout the day.
